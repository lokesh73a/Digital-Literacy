Stay Safe Online Checklist
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone
Always verify website URLs before entering login details
Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts
Avoid clicking links from unknown emails or messages
Use trusted and updated antivirus software
Regularly update your apps and operating system
For UPI safety: Never approve unknown payment requests
For UPI safety: Double-check receiver details before sending money
Report suspicious activity immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
Call 1930 in case of financial fraud
