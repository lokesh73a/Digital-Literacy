# Digital-Literacy-Project
Digital Literacy Portfolio project demonstrating online presence, collaboration tools, email etiquette, and cyber safety awareness.

## Project Overview  
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio developed as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University.  

As a Student Digital Ambassador, this project reflects my understanding of key digital skills required in today’s academic and professional environments. It focuses on areas such as digital awareness, online safety, professional communication, use of collaborative tools, and cybercrime prevention.  

The portfolio is divided into five structured tasks, each aligned with a module of the course and supported with practical work, screenshots, and written reflections.

## Task Summary  

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic  
Created a one-page infographic using Canva covering topics such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. The design focuses on clarity, visual appeal, and easy understanding.

---

### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio  
Set up and updated professional profiles on platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn. These profiles form the foundation of my digital presence and will be used to showcase skills, projects, and achievements over time.

---

### Task 3 – Platforms Exploration  
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge to build problem-solving skills. Additionally, created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy and linked it to a response sheet to understand collaboration and data collection tools.

---

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette  
Drafted professional emails including a request for assignment extension and an internship inquiry. Also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible and professional online communication.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness  
Developed a case study on phishing, explaining how such attacks occur and their impact. Created a prevention checklist with practical steps to help students stay safe online, including financial and UPI safety tips.
