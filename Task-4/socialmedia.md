#### DO's

1. Avoid spreading false or misleading content.
2. Be respectful to others
3. Communicate politely and professionally.
4. Build a positive online presence
5. Share useful, educational, or professional content.

#### Don'ts

1. They may contain malware or scams.
2. Don’t overshare your life
3. Excessive sharing can compromise your privacy.
4. Don’t post inappropriate content.
5. It can negatively affect your reputation.
